About InfraGuard

Next-Gen Infrastructure Monitoring

InfraGuard is an advanced IoT-based infrastructure monitoring system developed to ensure structural safety and environmental stability in real-time. Our system integrates multiple sensors including MPU6050 for tilt detection, DHT11 for temperature and humidity, LDR for light intensity, PIR for motion, and HC-SR04 for distance measurement.

All sensor data is processed by an ESP32 microcontroller and displayed on our web dashboard with real-time updates, interactive charts, and automatic alerts. The system automatically calculates risk levels based on tilt angles, temperature thresholds, and proximity alerts.
